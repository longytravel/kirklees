- #1 Sixth Form College in North of England
- 2,700+ students
- Sentiment score estimate: 9.2/10

**Bradford College** (primary FE competitor)

## 5. Recommendations for Demo

1. **Primary sentiment metric:** Use RateMyApprenticeship (8.9/10, 83 reviews) as the headline quantitative score - it has the most reviews and is apprenticeship-specific
2. **Ofsted as quality benchmark:** All institutions have Ofsted ratings enabling direct comparison
3. **Review aggregation:** Combine Trustpilot, WhatUni, Uni Compare, and RateMyApprenticeship for a composite view
4. **Competitive positioning:** Kirklees College is mid-tier among local competitors, with outstanding apprenticeship provision but behind Greenhead academically
5. **Social media monitoring:** Facebook (14K) and Instagram (8.3K) are the primary channels to track
6. **Gap:** Google Maps reviews need manual collection; StudentCrowd has no profile; FE Choices data is discontinued
