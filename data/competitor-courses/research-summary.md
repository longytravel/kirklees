## Key Competitive Findings

### Kirklees College's Unique Strengths
1. **Only T Level provider in Kirklees** - 8 T Level programmes with strong industry placements
2. **Specialist facilities** - Dedicated centres for Engineering, Construction, Animal Care, Process Manufacturing
3. **Dual-town presence** - Huddersfield and Dewsbury campuses serve wider borough
4. **Scale** - Largest FE provider in Kirklees with 10,000+ students
5. **Employer partnerships** - Lloyds Banking Group, Jet2, and hundreds of local employers

### Competitive Threats
1. **A-Level dominance by sixth forms** - Greenhead (Outstanding, 99% pass) and HNC own the academic pathway
2. **Apprenticeship awards going to rivals** - Bradford College won National Apprenticeship Provider of Year 2026; Calderdale has Outstanding apprenticeships
3. **Mixed programme gap** - HNC is the only Kirklees college offering combined A-Level + BTEC
4. **Ofsted gap** - Two nearby competitors (Greenhead, Barnsley) hold Outstanding vs Kirklees Good

### Subject Area Overlaps (High Competition)
- Engineering, Construction, Health & Social Care, Business, Motor Vehicle, Hair & Beauty, Hospitality & Catering

### Kirklees Differentiators (Low Competition)
- T Levels (sole Kirklees provider)
- Animal Care / Land-based Studies (Taylor Hill Centre)
- Process Manufacturing (dedicated centre)
- Plant Machinery Operations

### Potential Curriculum Gaps to Investigate
- Dental Nursing (Bradford offers this)
- Pharmacy Dispensing (Bradford apprenticeship)
- Textiles/Fashion (Bradford degree-level)
- Performing Arts (Wakefield and Leeds are strong)
- Law (Leeds City College)
